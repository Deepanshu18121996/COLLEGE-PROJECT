About Project

The idea behind this project

A project begins with ideas, then they are formed in steps. Many of the initial steps can be inexpensive or free. Being Students, idea of our project "BookStor-An online used book selling website" came into our mind. The idea was to provide books to the students with economical weaker background by selling the books at very nominal cost & also buying their used books.

Our project is a website that work acts as a central book store, so that the user visiting the website can see a wide range of books arranged in respective categories. Bookstor provides students an oppotunity to find a place where they can find books of their use. BookStor is not just an e-commerce website, it's an another type of sales channel or a complementary traditional store.

The initiative to build this project has been taken by the team of 4 members. We tried to build a project that gives book lovers a platform where they can explore their desires,their need, their aspirations which can help them in many ways to build their career.
